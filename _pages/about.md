I think aloud on the internet.

This blog is powered by [fastpages](https://github.com/fastai/fastpages).

I write essays @ [my other blog](https://www.mrogers.london/). At some point I hope to marry the two, in a minimal style similar to [this](https://danromero.org/how-this-website-works.html).

Curious about everything. From economic history to capital allocation; from complexity science to behavioural science; from analytics to growth; from startups to banking. Attempting to build a lattice work of mental models.

Trying to stay at the edge of chaos. 

> "The edge of chaos is the constantly shifting battle zone between stagnation and anarchy, the one place where a complex system can be spontaneous, adaptive, and alive." - M. Waldrop

Often talk nonsense. Writing helps clarify my thinking. Suffer from information anxiety.
